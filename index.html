Brazillian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art and combat sport based on ground fighting and submission holds. BJJ focuses on the skill of taking an opponent to the ground, controlling one's opponent, gaining a dominant position, and using a number of techniques to force them into submission via joint locks or chokeholds.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, sometimes referred to as "Thai boxing," is a martial art and combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques. This discipline is known as the "art of eight limbs," as it is characterised by the combined use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins

Wrestling

Wrestling is a combat sport performed on a circular mat. Participants are required to use their bodies to execute a variety of different moves to pin their opponent and/or score points in order to win the fight. Wrestling focuses on holds, throws, takedowns, and gaining control of your opponent via clinches and ground control.

Boxing

Known traditionally as the "Sweet science", Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand wraps and mouthguards, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring. Victories in boxing can come fron knockouts or by scoring more points that your opponent during the bout.

Yoga

Yoga is a group of physical, mental, and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India and aim to control and still the mind. Yoga is one of the best classes to do for those looking to increase their flexibility, and has also been shown to be effective in gaining mental strength and confidence

MMA

Incorportating elements from Brazillian jiu jitsu, boxing, muay thai, wrestling and yoga, mixed martial arts (sometimes referred to as cage fighting or ultimate fighting) is a full-contact combat sport based on striking, grappling and ground fighting, incorporating techniques from various combat sports from around the world.
